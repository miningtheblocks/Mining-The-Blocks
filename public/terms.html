2. Nature of the Service

Mining The Blocks is a cooperative online game of chance with real-money prizes. The game world consists of a 201×201×201-block cube (8,102,601 blocks total) organized in 101 layers and divided into chains of 10 sequential episodes each.

Players use "picks" (mining turns) to break blocks. Each block may contain a prize gem or nothing. Prize distribution is governed by a deterministic algorithm whose logic is publicly documented. The algorithm is unpredictable to the individual player because the prize placement depends on a combination of server ID, layer, block number, and player count — information not practically combinable in real time.

Participation does not guarantee any prize or financial return. The entry fee is the price of participation, not a purchase of a specific prize.
